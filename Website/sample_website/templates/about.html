About Us.

Welcome to Mellow Tales, where magical adventures come to life through the pages of our storybooks! At MellowTales, we believe that every child deserves to experience the joy of reading and the wonder of storytelling. From whimsical fairy tales to thrilling adventures, our collection of storybooks offers something for every young reader to enjoy.

Mellow Tales helps children with increasing their attention span as when the child starts reading a certain storybook, our eye tracking and facial recognition technology tracks the engaging time of the child with the storybook screen and sends the engagement report to the parents.

At Mellow Tales, we are a team of five individuals coming from various disciplines with the goal of making learning and education fun and engaging for the budding readers.
